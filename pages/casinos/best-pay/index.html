What is RTP?

RTP percentage, or return to player percentage, is the amount of money the casino returns to the players in the form of winnings in the long run. Simply put, if you wager CA$100, and the RTP of a game is 96.5%, you will receive CA$96.5 back.

This explanation, however, oversimplifies the reality because the RTP is a number that is true over thousands of attempts. So, while it is essential to choose the highest payout online casino, it’s vital to acknowledge that the percentage only denotes the pure chance and not the real payout every single time and in every single game.

Average RTP at Online Casinos

Distinguishing between the particular game RTP and the casino's RTP can be a bit tricky, too. The online casino payout rate is the overall payout rate for all the games played during a period of time.

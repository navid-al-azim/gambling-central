Online Bingo Main Rules Explained

Bingo is not a complicated game. Players buy bingo cards with a printed set of numbers on them. Numbers are drawn at random and the players have to match their numbers to the ones drawn. The person who gets the first matching pattern wins a prize.

  • There are several bingo online variations of 90, 80, 75, and 30 balls and more
  • Winning patterns can differ – it can be horizontal, vertical, diagonal, etc.
  • Players can play the same game with one card or multiple cards
  • When a number is hit, players can mark the square by clicking on it or by selecting auto-daub
  • The payout depends on the wagers participating players make
  • Certain bingo games also offer a progressive jackpot

Understanding Bingo Patterns

There can be hundreds of different bingo patterns so it is impossible to list them all. While a horizontal line doesn’t require explanation, there are several special ones that we will list here. Don’t worry about keeping them in mind. In bingo games online, the computer will do the work for you: if they are hit, it will mark them off for you, and if you win, it will call bingo for you.

  • Four Corners

    This is an easy one to remember, as simply the four corners of one of your cards need to be marked off. This game can take a while as only four numbers need to be considered.

  • Clock Bingo

    This pattern is popular in chat bingo rooms. The pattern looks like a clock where the hour hand is between 4 and 5 o’clock. Players who get the winning pattern can win additional prizes in the chat room.

  • Wine Glass

    This pattern looks like a wine glass (or rather Martini glass) and is made up of 11 different numbers. Several different bingo games use this pattern.

  • Windmill Bingo

    Seventeen numbers are marked off on this pattern and it looks like a windmill from above. This pattern is played in 75-ball bingo. It can take a while to complete as so many numbers need to be marked off.

  • Bell Bingo

    This shape of a bell pattern is particularly popular around Christmas time but you can come across it any time of the year. Fourteen numbers are marked with this pattern and it is usually associated with progressive jackpot games.

  • Witches Hat

    This 13-number pattern resembles a Witches Hat and not surprisingly is very popular around Halloween. If you are playing chat bingo and announce the winning pattern through the chat room, you can win additional prizes with it.

All about Odds, RTP & House Edge

Bingo attracts many players because there is a possibility to win huge prizes with a small wager. However, in bingo only very few people win and the rest will lose, just like with slot machines.

The house edge in bingo is around 10% that is very much higher than what video poker or blackjack offers.

Bingo is a game of chance and players can’t influence the outcome, but playing a definite type of bingo at exact times, buying a certain amount of cards, and avoiding side bets can improve the chance of winning. You can test your odds by playing free bingo online before you make your first wager.

Bingo strategies That Might Help You

Mathematicians have been trying to come up with strategies that work in the long run – based on thousands or millions of draws. Bingo is a lottery type of game where no decisions are made by the player during the game but picking bingo cards with certain numbers might improve your chances according to statistics.

Strategy 1: Granville’s system

This system is based on the idea that the numbers should be as different from one another as possible. According to Granville, every number has an equal chance to be drawn so you should choose a variety of numbers:

  • As many high numbers as small numbers – it is unlikely that in a 90-ball game all numbers will be under 45
  • As many odd numbers as even numbers – it is unlikely that all numbers will be even
  • Numbers with different last digits – it is unlikely that 17, 27, 57, 67, and 87 will all be drawn

This system works over millions of draws, however, within the duration of a few games, it might not apply.

Strategy 2: Tippet’s system

This strategy, which was worked out by a British statistician, also mainly works in the long run. Tippet’s theory is that numbers tend to concentrate around the median of the numbers, so 45 in the 90-ball game and 38 in the 75-ball bingo.

  • Choose numbers closest to the middle number
  • This theory works best with longer games – don’t use it for quick 30-ball games
  • In 90-ball bingo choose mainly between 35 and 55
  • In 75-ball bingo choose mainly between 25 and 50

In the long run, this system makes sense so why not play bingo online free for as many rounds as you would like and see how it works out.
